Los Angeles

Los Angeles is known as the entertainment capital of the world, with an endless amount of things to do. You've definitely seen the beauty of L.A in many T.V shows and movies, but there is nothing like taking a journey in one of the biggest cities of California.

WHERE TO GO

Griffith Observatory

Known as "Southern California's gateway to the cosmos", people can view live shows in the Planetarium, enjoy the beautiful view of Los Angeles, or look through the night with a telescope.

Hollywood Walk of Fame

Are you a big fan of the Backstreet Boys? Mariah Carey? Johnny Depp? Talk a walk at the Hollywood Walk of Fame and browse the many stars that sit beneath your feet of your favorite musicians, actors, and much more!

WHERE TO EAT

Papilles

Get a taste of France at one of the best French restaurants in Los Angeles. Offering a huge selection of foods like pastas, salads, oysters, and much more!

Pizzana

In the mood for some pizza? Pizzana isn't just like any other regular pizza place in Los Angeles, they offer a great selection of specialty pizzas, pastas, and wine!

WHERE TO SHOP

The Grove

Often reffered to as the best shopping and dining experience in all of Los Angeles, The Grove offers places to shop at like Apple, Barnes and Nobles, and other well known retailers!

The Santee Alley

Looking for a unique outdoor shopping experience? Head to the Santee Alley where you can find over 150 retailers offering apparel, toys, perfumes, and much more!
