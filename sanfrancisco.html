San Francisco

San Francisco is often called "Everybodyâ€™s Favorite City," a title earned by its impressive scenery, cultural attractions, diversity, and world-class cuisine. San Francisco is filled with some of the best landmarks in the world like the Golden Gate Bridge and Alcatraz.

WHERE TO GO

Pier 39

A popular tourist attraction consisting of restaurants, rides, street performances, shops, and much more! Pier 39 should be at the top of your list of places to visit when in San Francisco.

Alcatraz Island

An offshore island that was originally a federal prison to some of the most dangerous prisoners in the United States. Take a ferry ride to the island day or night and explore the island at your own pace.

WHERE TO EAT

Super Duper Burgers

Looking for the best burgers in the city? The name brings on exactly what it says, taste anything from their "Ground Daily Natural Vegetarian" burgers to their "Chipotle Aioli" chicken sandwiches.

Pearl 6101

The best Mediterranean-inspired food that you can find in San Francisco, Pearl 16101 offers anything from seafood like their specialty cocktails to pasta like their "Ricotta Ravioli".

WHERE TO SHOP

Westfield San Francisco Centre

9 levels of over 170 shops and restaurants, there isn't anything that you can't find at the Westfield San Francisco Centre.

Union Square

A plaza located in downtown San Francisco, Union Square offers anything shopping, dining, and travel related.
