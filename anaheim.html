Anaheim

As a city built on imagination, Anaheim is where every unique and personal experience is sure to delight and inspire, how can you miss out on the perfect experience of taking a stroll through the wonderful city of Anaheim?

WHERE TO GO

Disneyland

Disneyland or "the happiest place on earth", is no stranger to the world. With over 53 attractions, truly experience an amazing time with the family and never lose that smile.

Disney California Adventure

Just a 30 seconds walk from Disneyland, visit another amazing Disney theme park with over 37 attractions!

WHERE TO EAT

Bubba Gump Shrimp Co.

Bubba Gump Shrimp Co. is no stranger to seafood lovers, serving not only the best seafood in Anaheim but also foods like burgers and soups.

The Pizza Press

Build your very own pizza in at Anaheim's most recognized pizza spot "The Pizza Press".

WHERE TO SHOP

Anaheim Plaza

9 levels of over 170 shops and restaurants, there isn't anything that you can't find at the Westfield San Francisco Centre.

Anaheim Town Square

Anaheim Town Square offers a great selection of shops, restaurants, and gyms including Gamestop, Chipotle, KFC, Ross, and much more.
