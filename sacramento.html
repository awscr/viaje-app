Sacramento

Sacramento is the capital of California and the 5th largest city in the state. It is located in north/central California, approximately an hour and a half northeast of Oakland and San Francisco. There are so many things to do in the city of trees!

WHERE TO GO

Old Sacramento

A riverfront historic district with over 125 shopping, dining, events, and attractions, there is no other destination quite like the Old Sacramento Waterfront.

California State Capitol Museum

Home to the government of the U.S state of California, the California State Capitol building also serves as a museum. Experience California's rich history and witness the making of history through the modern lawmaking process.

WHERE TO EAT

South

It's pretty hard not to hear about one of Sacramento's greatest burger spots, South offers anything from chicken to sandwiches/burgers.

The Boiling Crab

One of the greatest seafood spots in all of California, The Boiling Crab offers anything seafood like shrimp, king crab, clams, and even chicken nuggets.

WHERE TO SHOP

Arden Fair

Arden Fair is the largest shopping mall in Sacramento, with over 165 stores including Disney, Apple, Macy's, and many more.

DOCO (Downtown Commons)

DOCO is where the tourists and locals like to hang out, a two story mixed-use enterntainment and shopping complex, with many retailers and restaurants to choose from.
