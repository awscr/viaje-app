San Jose

Nicknamed the capital of Silicon Valley, San Jose is a diverse area with an innovative spirit. While it has a distinct downtown, San Jose is a sprawling metro area that is as much defined by its suburban neighborhoods and large tech campuses as it is by the high-rises in its business district.

WHERE TO GO

Winchester Mystery Museum

Once the home to Sarah Winchester, the widow of firearm magnate William Wirt Winchester, experience one of the most mysteriously haunted places in the United States!

The Tech Interactive

The Tech Interactive is a science and technology center located in downtown San Jose. How can you visit Silicon Valley and not discover the many technologies that this place has to offer!

WHERE TO EAT

The Boiling Crab

One of the greatest seafood spots in all of California, The Boiling Crab offers anything seafood like shrimp, king crab, clams, and even chicken nuggets.

Pizza Antica

Check out one of the best traditional pizza spots in San Jose, Pizza Antica offers foods served with the outmost top-quality ingredients.

WHERE TO SHOP

Santana Row

Santana Row is Silicon Valley's premiere destination for shopping, dining, living, and much more. Take a look at over 70 shops, 20 restaurants, and 9 spas and salons.

Westfield Valley Fair

With over 273 shops and restaurants, there isn't anything that you can't find at the Westfield Valley Fair shopping mall.
